Coffee

I’m not sure why I’m writing this; maybe just to share on this site.

“Love is like coffee and sugar,

you don’t fall for the sweetness first,

but for the warmth and comfort it brings.

Over time, the perfect blend becomes clear,

and though I could list a thousand reasons why,

none of them are about the surface, only about the way

we fit together.”
