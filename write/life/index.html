The Simple Path to a Fulfilling Life

2024-08-09 by Amritxyz

Its not that complicated, its not easy, but it is simple.

  1. Accept yourself as someone valuable unconditionally :

    • Accept yourself as a valuable and worth-while Human Being.
      Don’t be the type of person who doesn’t feel they are good enough.
      Recognize that you’re put here on this earth for a specific purpose,
      A valuable purpose. God doesn’t make anybody extra.
      God don’t make no junk. There is nobody on this earth that is here
      For no reason. Everybody has a purpose and your purpose is to find
      Your purpose. Your purpose is to find your mission. The place where
      Can make a significant contribution.

  2. Accept the responsibility of your actions :

    • Accept total responsibility for everything that you are,
      And everything that you become.
      Which means no Blaming, no excuses.

  3. Learn to forgive yourself and others without grudges :

    • The key to kingdom of heaven is forgiveness the key to
      All spiritual development straight gate is forgiveness,
      the ability to freely forgive. No grudges, no anger,
      no frustration, just let go up all grudges against you.
      That’s why it says in lord’s prayer.
      ‘Forgive us our sins, as we forgive those who sin against us.’

  4. Feed your mind with positive and loving thought :

    • Don’t feed your mind with mental candy, mental junk.
      Feed you mind with the kinds of though that are consistent
      with you becoming very best person you can be possibly be.

  5. Set noble goals and work towards them :

    • Say to yourself yeah these are the values, these the goal
      these the ideals of the very good person that you can possibly be.

  6. Take care of your body :

    • Remember the body is the temple of the spirit.
