My Two Faves

2025-06-09 by Amritxyz

I strum the strings, but they don’t sound right. Like my heart when you walk in all shaky and tight.

I practice chords for hours, trying to get it clean. But you smile, and I forget the song I’ve ever seen.

You don’t care if I mess up. You laugh, and say, “Again!” And somehow, with you near, even wrong notes feel like rain soft, and kind of sweet, like a secret in the air.

I love my guitar, it’s my old, wooden friend. But I love you more— you’re the song without an end.

You’re the tune I can’t learn, but keep trying every night. You’re the reason I play, and the reason I write.
