My Two Faves

2025-06-09

i strum the strings,
but they don’t sound right.
like my heart when you walk in,
it beats too fast inside.

i practice chords for hours,
trying to get it clean.
but you smile, and i forget,
the song i’ve ever seen.

you don’t care if i mess up,
you laugh, and say, “again!”.
and somehow, with you near,
even wrong notes feel like rain.

i love my guitar,
it’s my old, wooden friend.
but i love you more,
you’re the song without an end.

you’re the tune i can’t learn,
but keep trying every night.
you’re the reason
i play,
and the reason
i write.
