A NULL Romance

2025-08-10

I’m a void pointer, no type at all,
I can be anything, but I’m nothing’s call.
I’m looking for meaning, searching for you,
But I don’t even know what I’m supposed to do.

You’re a null pointer, pointing to nothing,
Empty by design, you hold no thing.
We’re both incomplete in our own way,
Trying to find what we’re meant to say.

We drift through memory, nowhere to go,
Two broken pointers, that’s all we know.
If we could connect, if we could align,
Maybe we’d become something that’s fine.

But now we’re just lost in the empty space,
A void and a null in this lonely place.
We’re lost, but maybe it’s true
That’s exactly why I need you.
