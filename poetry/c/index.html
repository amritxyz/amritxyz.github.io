A NULL Romance

2025-08-10 by Amritxyz

I’m a void pointer, lost and undefined, Roaming through code, no type to bind. I’m searching for meaning, something to claim, But without her, i stay the same.
She’s a null pointer, quiet and still, Empty, waiting, yet free at will. I wander through the lines, incomplete, Hoping one day, our paths will meet.
Together, we could be more than this, A perfect match, a type we’d bliss. But for now, i drift in endless loops, Me, the void, and her, the null truth.
